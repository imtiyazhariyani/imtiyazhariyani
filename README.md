## 🧬 About me

I am a PhD candidate at **UC Irvine** working at the intersection of **evolutionary genomics, reproductive biology, and computational biology**. My PhD research focuses on how gene and transposable element expression evolve across cell types, especially in the _Drosophila_ germline.

I enjoy building reproducible pipelines, analyzing high-dimensional biological data, and translating complex genomic patterns into clear biological stories.

---

## 🔬 Current focus

* Single-cell and single-nucleus RNA-seq analysis
* Transposable element expression at family and locus resolution
* Comparative genomics across species and strains
* Bioinformatics tooling for scalable biological discovery

### 🧬 Transposable elements

Family-level and locus-resolved TE expression analysis using single-cell and bulk genomic data.

### 🧪 Chromatin and epigenomics

Pipelines, visualization workflows, statistical modeling, and clean scientific reporting.
